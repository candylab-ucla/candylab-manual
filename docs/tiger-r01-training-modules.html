UCLA CANDY Lab Manual

Section 5 TIGER R01 Training Modules

5.1 Introduction to TIGER R01

  1. Read TIGER R01 Protocol Paper
  2. Complete RA Exclusion Criteria Written Assignment
  3. Review TIGER R01 Study Design PPT
  4. Review TIGER R01 Timepoint visualization
  5. Review TIGER R01 Measure visualizations
  6. Quiz: TIGER R01 Protocol

5.2 Recruitment

  1. Review Recruitment MOP
  2. Review TIGER FAQs document
  3. Set-up and post on recruitment social media profiles.
  4. Complete in-lab recruitment posting tasks.
  5. Complete off-campus recruitment tasks.
  6. Review how to use recruitment tracker

5.3 Consent Calls

  1. Read all consent & assent forms
  2. Observe a consent call and conduct a mock consent with another lab member
  3. Conduct control consent call with coordinator direct supervision.
  4. Conduct MDD consent call with coordinator direct supervision
  5. Quiz: Understanding of consent procedures

5.4 Data Management

  1. Review all data management (pre-V1, V1, and V2) MOPs and trackers
  2. V1 and V2 Data Checks on REDCap
  3. Complete data management tasks under supervision
  4. Quiz: Trackers, CRFs, Subject Data Folders

5.5 Participant Scheduling

  1. Complete portions of V1 and V2 scheduling CRF with close supervision
  2. Call and schedule at least two participant visits (with supervision)
  3. Complete V1 and V2 Scheduling CRF independently with coordinator review (repeat as needed)
  4. Quiz: Understanding of scheduling procedures

5.6 Eligibility Screener Calls

  1. Review eligibility screener MOP and script
  2. Watch KSADS Training videos
  3. Observe REDCap notes for during an eligibility screener call
  4. Individual and group mock eligibility screeners

5.7 V2 Trier Judge & Admin RA

  1. TSST training
  2. Autovoxel quiz
  3. Biospecimen collection training
  4. Coggies demonstration training
  5. Review select tasks from V2 CRF
